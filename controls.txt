Open Hand - Start Timer/Resume
Close Hand - Pause Timer

Two Open Hands - New Timer
Two Close Hands - Remove Timer
Hand Pointing - Scroll through timers


When timer hasn't started yet
Swipe Left/Right - scroll through preset times
01:00, 02:00, 03:00, 05:00, 10:00, 20:00, 30:00, 1:00:00, 2:00:00 
(you can also type in a time)

When timer is started already
Swipe Left - -30 sec
Swipe Right - + 30 sec
